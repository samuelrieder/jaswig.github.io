SeatUp

Jaswig's sit stand stool

The SeatUp was designed alongside the StandUp, around the same principles: simplicity and beauty!

Standing for a whole day is not what we aim for at Jaswig. Your body is made to move and change positions! The StandUp is designed to be a high work surface because, at a high work surface you can both sit and stand. With the SeatUp, active sitting and changing from sitting to leaning to standing is a matter of seconds.
