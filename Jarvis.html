Jarvis™ Standing Desk

Sustainable, versatile and spacious

Designed to make you move. The Jarvis couldn’t reflect our company’s tagline better. It’s spacious, sustainable bamboo work surface is extremely pleasant to work on.

The frame of the Jarvis is available in different colours and has great accessories to help you be at your best.
