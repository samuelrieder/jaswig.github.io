Are standing desks good

Our bodies are not made to do something (whatever it is) for too long at a time. Our bodies are made to change in positions and to move around a lot. The best position is always the next position, this is core in the ergonomic studies. A standing desk promotes just that.

How can a standing desk help

A standing desks makes your work environment a lot more dynamic. It makes sure that you'll move around more often. A sitting desk stimulates people to reside in a certain position for a very long time. Sitting makes people tired and thus stimulates them to remain where they are. A standing desk draws attention and makes people choose to stand up and move. This is why someone should consider a standing desk. There needs to be a healthy balance between sitting, moving and standing.

  • Burn more calories Standing desks make people move around more and switch positions.
  • Take pressure from your spine because sitting exerts a lot of pressure on the discs present in the spine
  • Activate more and big muscle groupsstanding and moving around activates big muscle groups
  • Boost your blood flowbecause large muscle groups are activated
  • Makes you more efficientbecause blood flow increases which keeps you awake

After having experimented with standing desks one could start to increase his/her time behind a standing desk by placing a high stool behind the desk. This way you can easily hop on and off the stool and change positions even more.

Quick wrap up:

  • standing takes away pressure from the discs in-between your spine, a footrest is essential for this kind of position
