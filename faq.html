New to Jaswig

Jaswig helps you to move more, because when you move, you feel more present and alive. The idea behind our company was born out of back and neck pain. Some of us started working, others were still studying. But we all shared the same sitting hours and its concequences. That’s why we decided to bundle our forces and devise a solution. This was back in 2015, when the first StandUp was designed.

In 2017 we partnered with the B-Corp Fully. We broadened our product portfolio with their top-products. Next to this, Fully will further assist Jaswig in its goal to get people standing and moving during work. Their 15 years of experience in the furniture market will play a crucial role!

We used to be part of 1% for the planet but have paused our subscription due to some internal reoganisation. We hope to be a part of 1% again soon though! 1% is an organization that was cofounded by Yvon Chouinard, the founder of the outdoor clothing company Patagonia. He is a strong believer that you can use business to do good things for the world. We are part of 1% for the planet which means that 1% of our revenue will go to non profit organizations that are creating a healthier planet.



This year we decided to team up with Natuurpunt, we are going to buy a piece of land together and replant native trees.

The Cooper standing desk converter comes fully assembled. This means you can just take it out of the box and start using it right away.

The Cooper standing desk converter is a product that transforms the normal height desk you already own (and don't want to get rid of, which is great thinking :)).

So you actually just have to place the Cooper on your desk and there you go, you now have an adjustable standing desk.

An important part of the Cooper is the adjustable gas spring with which you can adjust the resistance/help the spring gives when the desk is moving up and down. For example when you have two screens mounted on your Cooper, there is a lot of weight on it. This means the spring has to give a lot of power when you want to move the desk up as otherwise you have to put that energy into moving it up and down. The adjustable spring makes the process of moving the desk up and down as effortless as possible.

So once you've adjusted the spring for your specific setup, you can adjust the desk in height by pressing the flap on the right side of the Cooper. Once the flap is pressed, you can move the desk up or down to the exact height you want.
